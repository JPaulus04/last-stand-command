Frequently Asked Questions

01How do I restore my purchases after reinstalling the game?›
Open Last Stand Command and navigate to Settings → Store. Tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you are signed in to the same Apple ID used for the original purchase. Your items should restore within a few seconds. If they don't appear, force-close the app, reopen it, and try again.
02The game crashes on launch — what should I do?›
First, make sure you're running the latest version of Last Stand Command from the App Store. Fully close the app and relaunch. If the problem persists, try restarting your device. If crashes continue, please contact us with your device model and iOS version.
03I was charged but didn't receive my in-app purchase. What now?›
First, use the Restore Purchases option in Settings → Store. All transactions go through Apple — if the issue persists, please report it at reportaproblem.apple.com with your order ID. You can also contact us and we'll help resolve it.
04My progress was lost. Can it be recovered?›
If you had iCloud sync enabled, your progress may be stored in the cloud — sign in with the same Apple ID and re-enable iCloud in the game settings. If iCloud was not enabled, progress stored locally may not be recoverable after an uninstall. We strongly recommend enabling iCloud backup going forward.
05How do I turn off notifications?›
Go to your device's Settings → Notifications → Last Stand Command and toggle off Allow Notifications. You can also manage notification preferences from within the game's Settings menu.
06Is an internet connection required to play?›
Last Stand Command supports offline play for most modes. An internet connection is required for leaderboard updates, cloud saving, and online features. Some content may be temporarily unavailable without a connection.
07Does the game collect personal data?›
We collect only minimal data needed to operate the game (such as anonymous crash reports and game progress). We do not sell your data to third parties. Please see our Privacy Policy below for full details.
08How do I request a refund?›
All refund requests must go through Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, find the transaction, and select "Request a Refund." Apple will review and respond within a few days.

Legal & Privacy

Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

Last updated: 2025


Information We Collect
Last Stand Command collects anonymous gameplay data (level progress, session length) and device information (model, OS version) solely for improving the game and diagnosing crashes. We do not collect names, email addresses, or personally identifiable information through the game.


In-App Purchases
All purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not store or have access to your payment information.


Third-Party Services
We may use Apple's GameCenter and iCloud services for leaderboards and cloud saves. Their respective privacy policies apply to those services.


Data Retention & Deletion
Anonymous analytics data is retained for up to 12 months. You may request deletion of any data by contacting us via the support form on this page.


Children's Privacy
This game is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
