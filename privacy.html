Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026  |  Effective Immediately
01 //

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Last Stand Command ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you play our iOS game. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

By downloading and playing Last Stand Command, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

02 //

Information We Collect

We collect only the minimal data necessary to operate and improve the game:

  • Anonymous gameplay data — level progress, session length, in-game actions
  • Device information — device model, iOS version, language settings
  • Crash reports — anonymous logs to help us diagnose and fix bugs

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, or any other personally identifiable information through the game itself.

03 //

In-App Purchases

All purchases within Last Stand Command are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information, credit card details, or billing address at any time.

For questions about billing or refunds, please visit reportaproblem.apple.com.

04 //

Third-Party Services

Last Stand Command may use the following Apple services, each governed by Apple's own Privacy Policy:

  • Apple GameCenter — for leaderboards and achievements
  • iCloud — for optional game progress sync across your devices
  • App Store — for all purchase processing

We do not use third-party advertising networks or sell your data to any third parties.

05 //

Data Retention & Deletion

Anonymous analytics data is retained for a maximum of 12 months, after which it is automatically deleted.

You may request deletion of any data associated with your game account at any time by contacting us through our support form. We will process all deletion requests within 30 days.

06 //

Children's Privacy

Last Stand Command is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately via our support form and we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

07 //

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of Last Stand Command after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions about this policy?

Contact our support team and we'll respond within 24 hours.
